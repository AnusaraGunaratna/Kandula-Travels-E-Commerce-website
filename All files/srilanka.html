Sri Lanka.


Sri Lanka, formerly known as Ceylon and officially the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka is island country situated in South Asia. Sri Lanka lies in the Indian Ocean southwest to Bay of Bengal also known as the Pearl of Indian Ocean. The population of Sri Lanka is around 22 million and is many different culutures and Languages. The majority of the nation's population is Sinhalese followed by Tamils.

Sri Lanka is a beautiful country with a lots of breathtaking locations and beautiful animals. It is the best safari destination outside of africa. Sri Lanka's most popular safary animals are Elephant, Leopard, Sloth Bear, Blue and Sperm Whale.
Not only the wildlife, Sri Lanka is also famous for it's heritage and the unbelievable places that are built by ancient Sri lankans.
So Visit Sri Lanka to witness the full experience.
